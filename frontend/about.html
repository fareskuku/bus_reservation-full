Our Mission

Addis Metro is dedicated to providing affordable, reliable, and convenient bus transportation within Addis Ababa. We aim to make city travel accessible for everyone with our modern bus service.

Why Choose Us

We cover all major areas of Addis Ababa including Piazza, Bole, Mercato, Mexico Square, and more.

Buses run every 10-15 minutes during peak hours, ensuring you never wait long.
